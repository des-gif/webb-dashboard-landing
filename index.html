Questions

Good to know

Is this only for business owners?

No. It's useful for business owners, freelancers, consultants, managers, creators — anyone managing multiple projects or responsibilities.

Is this an ADHD tool?

It's ADHD-friendly, but it isn't a medical or clinical tool. It's designed to support focus, structure, planning and task management.

Does it replace accounting software?

No. It gives a simple day-to-day money overview. You should still use proper accounting tools or professional advice where needed.

Can I customise it?

Yes. You can edit sections, rename projects, add templates and adjust it to fit your own life and work.

Does it come with example data?

Yes. The customer version includes example templates and sample entries so you know exactly how to start.

Is your personal data included?

No. The customer version is a clean clone with no personal data from the original Webb CEO Dashboard.

Who is it best for?

People with lots of ideas, projects, tasks and responsibilities who need one simple place to organise everything.
