What it is

A personal command centre for business, money and mental load

Webb CEO Dashboard is a business and wellbeing operating dashboard for entrepreneurs, consultants, managers, creatives, neurodivergent business owners and busy professionals who need one calm place to organise their life, business, money, tasks, ideas and priorities.

Instead of managing everything across notebooks, apps, emails, screenshots and memory, it gives users one clear digital base — a command centre for daily structure, business organisation, financial awareness, project tracking, wellbeing check-ins and decision-making.

Why it is investable

A clear everyday problem in a growing market

Many people don't need another complicated app. They need one clear place to manage their day, money, ideas, projects and mental load — which is exactly the growing market for practical productivity, personal operating systems and wellbeing-aware work tools.

Investment, partnership & licensing

How to get involved

Webb CEO Dashboard is open to investment, partnership and licensing. Investment could support product development, design, automation, onboarding, templates, mobile-friendly features, marketing, video walkthroughs and customer support.

Partnership opportunities include business coaches, productivity and ADHD coaches, accountants, care consultants and small-business support organisations. Licensing could allow sector-specific versions for consultants, agencies or business communities — and the dashboard can be packaged with Local Business AI Hub, CareIQ, training products or coaching services.
