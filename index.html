Questions

Good to know

Is this only for business owners?

No. It's for business owners, freelancers, consultants, managers, creators — anyone carrying multiple projects and responsibilities who needs one calm place to organise it all.

Is this a wellbeing or a business tool?

Both — on purpose. It holds your business machinery and your wellbeing, because the two aren't separate in real life. It's supportive, but it isn't a medical or clinical tool.

Is the daily guidance financial, medical or therapy advice?

No. The guidance is general, practical, educational support to help you organise and take action. The financial guidance isn't regulated financial advice, the health and mental-health guidance isn't medical advice or therapy, and none of it diagnoses or treats anything. Please see a qualified professional where you need one — and if you ever feel unsafe or unable to cope, seek urgent support right away.

Does it replace accounting software?

No. It gives a clear day-to-day money overview so cash doesn't surprise you. You should still use proper accounting tools or professional advice where needed.

Can I customise it?

Yes. Edit sections, rename projects, add templates and shape it around your own life and work.

Does it come with example data?

Yes. The customer version includes example templates and sample entries so you know exactly how to start.

Is your personal data included?

No. The customer version is a clean clone with no personal data from the original Webb CEO Dashboard.
